Current PPI Model: Purdy v1 (Default)

How your PPI is calculated:

  • Distance: Total meters covered in your run
  • Time: Total elapsed time in seconds
  • Baseline Time: Elite performance time for your distance
  • Performance Ratio: Baseline Time ÷ Your Time
  • Purdy Formula: 1000 × (Baseline Time ÷ Your Time)^(-2.0)
  • Range: PPI is capped between 100 and 2000

  • 1500m: 3:30 → 1000 points
