ℹ️ PPI Transparency

Current PPI Model: Purdy v1 (Default)

How your PPI is calculated:

  • Distance: Total meters covered in your run
  • Time: Total elapsed time in seconds
  • Baseline Time: Elite performance time for your distance
  • Performance Ratio: Baseline Time ÷ Your Time
  • Purdy Formula: 1000 × (Baseline Time ÷ Your Time)^(-2.0)
  • Range: PPI is capped between 100 and 2000

Elite Baseline Anchors:

  • 1500m: 3:30 → 1000 points
  • 5000m: 12:35 → 1000 points
  • 10000m: 26:11 → 1000 points
  • Half Marathon: 59:00 → 1000 points
  • Marathon: 2:04:20 → 1000 points

Score Ranges:

  • Elite Performance: 1000 points (meets baseline)
  • Competitive Performance: 694 points (slower than elite)
  • Recreational Performance: 300-500 points
  • Moderate Performance: 100 points (minimum score)
